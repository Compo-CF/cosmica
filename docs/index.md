# Cosmica: Idle Universe

> Build your observatory empire across the cosmos. Tap, idle, ascend.

Cosmica is a relaxed idle game for iPhone. Start with a backyard telescope, grow to operate Dyson Swarms and Multiverse Receivers, and Big Bang your way to permanent multipliers across seven cosmic tiers.

## What's in the game

**Core loop.** Tap the cosmic orb to earn Stardust ✦. Buy generators that earn Stardust automatically — even when the app is closed. Offline earnings catch you up when you return.

**Seven cosmic tiers.** Stargazer → Astronomer → Astrophysicist → Cosmologist → Galactic Cartographer → Architect of the Void → Demiurge. Each tier grants a permanent earnings multiplier that survives even Big Bang resets.

**Big Bang prestige.** Hit the lifetime Stardust threshold and reset your generators for Cosmic Shards ◈ — each granting +2% permanent earnings, compounding across every prestige.

**12 generators, 36 upgrades.** From Backyard Telescope to Demiurge Engine. Three escalating upgrade tiers per generator plus two late-game global multipliers.

**No accounts. No analytics. No tracking by the developer.** Single-player idle. Your save lives on your device and in your private iCloud account. Apple StoreKit handles purchases; Google AdMob may serve ads if you haven't purchased Remove Ads.
